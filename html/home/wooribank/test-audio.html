FIRST BANK : Establish the legitimacy of national finance

In 1899, Emperor Gojong ordered the establishment of the ‘greatest bank under the sky’ to protect the property of the people and country

FIRST BANK : Establish the legitimacy of national finance
1899

Birth of a national bank

After the opening of the port, foreign banks including Japan began to threaten the domestic commercial supremacy as they entered Joseon. To cope with this, the merchants and bureaucrats received capital from Emperor Gojong. and established ‘Daehan Cheonil Bank (the greatest bank under the sky) - former Woori Bank’ on January 30.
FIRST BANK : Establish the legitimacy of national finance
1910~1945

Woori Bank, a protector of national finance

Established with national capital, Woori Bank stabilized the monetary and fiscal system against Japanese finance during the Japanese colonial period and played a role in defending the finance of Joseon by funding Joseon merchants to protect commercial supremacy
FIRST BANK : Establish the legitimacy of national finance
1945~1960

Woori Bank during the cataclysm period after liberation

During the Korean War after liberation, Woori Bank provided the evacuation funds to the customers to share the suffering of the people. In the late 1950s, it increased the efficiency of banking by introducing financial services and office equipment from abroad for new leap.
