1960~1990

Woori Bank, a reliable partner for Korea and Korean companies

In the 1960s, Woori Bank contributed to national economic development through active financial support for SMEs in order to respond to national policy to promote light industry.
Then, as the opening of the financial market expanded international financial services, it led the internationalization of the banking industry and the advancement of financial technology.
Best Bank : No. 1 bank representing Korea
1990~2019

Toward a great bank, beyond the best

Woori Bank overcame the financial crisis with the people in the late 1990s, and succeeded in privatization through an improvement in profitability and asset soundness, digital innovation, and global growth strategy.
Woori Bank has become a representative of Korean finance by being selected as the Best Bank by The Banker for three consecutive years starting in 2016. With the official launch of Woori Finance Holdings in January 2019, it is preparing for a great leap forward by becoming a financial group that goes beyond Korea’s best.
