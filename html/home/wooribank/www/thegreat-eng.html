THE GREATEST : GREAT BANK

Woori Bank Constellation
Each affiliate of Woori Financial Group becomes a star to constitute Aquarius and Capricorn, the constellations that mark the founding date of Woori Bank and the launch date of Woori Finance Holdings, respectively.
Great Bank : A global leading financial group spreading its wings throughout the world
Since its founding in 1899, Woori Bank has contributed to the economic development of Korea by working with customers, contributing to the development of the financial industry and fulfilling its social responsibilities. In the future, Woori Bank will lead the world of digital finance by training financial experts and ensure Woori becomes a global leading group based on the largest global network among Korean commercial banks.
